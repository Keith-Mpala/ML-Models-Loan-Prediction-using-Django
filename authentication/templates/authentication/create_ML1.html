About the dataset

Dataset is a subset of the LendingClub dataSet obtained from Kaggle. Click Here to access the dataset

Machine Learning Models used

Since our predictive variable is a Binary classification, therefore machine Learning algorithms used are Classifier algorithms such as XGBoost , CatBoost , Boosting Classifier & Logistic Regression .

Business Problem

Model predicts whether or not a borrower will pay back their loan. This help us to assess whether or not a potential customer is likely to pay back the loan. Our predicting variable is a binary classification of loan status, whether Fully Paid or Charge off (defaulted).
