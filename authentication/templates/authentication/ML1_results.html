Interpretation of Model results

  • A lower Probability (below 0.5) shows a high chance that Loan will be Charged Off (defaulted).

    • * Further action needs to be taken for such loans.
  • A higher Probability (above 0.65) shows a high chance that Loan will be Fully Paid.

    • * Periodic review of loan performance
  • A Probability around the mean (between 0.5 - 0.64)

    • * Close monitoring and evaluation of loan performance
